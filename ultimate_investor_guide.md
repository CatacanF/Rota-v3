## Understanding Reports

### Executive Summary
**What It Is**: AI-generated market overview  
**How to Use**: Quick pulse check before diving deeper  
**Key Info**: Market themes, risks, opportunities

### Market Sentiment
**Indicators**:
- **Compound Score**: -1.0 (very negative) to +1.0 (very positive)
- **Distribution**: % of positive/negative/neutral articles
- **Confidence**: How reliable the sentiment reading is

**Trading Implications**:
- **Positive + High Confidence**: Risk-on environment
- **Negative + High Confidence**: Consider hedging
- **Neutral**: Wait for clearer signals

## Advanced Features

### Portfolio Analyzer

**Metrics Provided**:
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted returns
- **Max Drawdown**: Worst peak-to-trough decline
- **VaR 95%**: Daily loss potential (95% confidence)
- **Concentration Risk**: Portfolio diversification

**How to Improve Sharpe Ratio**:
1. Reduce volatility (diversify)
2. Increase returns (better stock selection)
3. Cut losers faster
4. Let winners run

**Managing Drawdown**:
- Set max drawdown limit (e.g., 15%)
- Use stop-losses
- Reduce position sizes in high-risk environments

### Alert Engine

**Alert Types**:
1. **Price Alerts**: Stock moved >5%
2. **Volume Alerts**: Unusual activity detected
3. **News Alerts**: Breaking events
4. **Calendar Alerts**: Economic event approaching
5. **Risk Alerts**: Portfolio metrics exceeded

**Best Practices**:
- Acknowledge critical alerts immediately
- Review warning alerts daily
- Adjust alert thresholds as needed

### Sector Rotation

**How It Works**:
- Tracks 6 major sectors
- Ranks by performance
- Identifies rotation trends

**Trading Strategy**:
- **Strong Rotation**: Shift to leading sectors
- **Moderate Rotation**: Hold current positions
- **Low Rotation**: Market breadth weak, reduce risk
